India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia.  It is the seventh-largest country by area, the second-most populous country, and it is bordered by Pakistan to the west; China, Nepal, and Bhutan to the northeast; and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. India is situated in the northern hemisphere, mostly north of the Tropic of Cancer, with the Tropic of Cancer passing through the middle of the country.
